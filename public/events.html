Murky Forest '22

15/09/22

St Catherine's Hill, Dorset

Rob, Fergus and the Murky Sonics crew are proud to present to you our debut festival - Murky Forest ’22. On the 15th-18th September, we’ll be setting up camp just outside Bournemouth for a weekend of music and entertainment - taking you from the soft melodies of jazz to the harsh low-end of dubstep. The private festival will take place from Thursday through to Sunday, with 3 days and 3 nights of music. Catering for the weekend will be provided by our very own Murky Chef, Finn. He will be providing you a light brunch each day, with a large themed meal each evening. There will be a heavily stocked, reasonably priced bar. However, the only currency accepted will be Murky Mulla. In the daytime, the focus will be on the outdoor stage and sporting arena, with lots of incredible live musicians heading down for the weekend. There will also be 5-a-side football and darts tournaments throughout the weekend, with some saucy prizes for the champions.

MS004 w/ Idioteque, Delian Sound

20/06/22

MASH, Cambridge

Following a sold-out 003 event last term, we're heading back to MASH for a huge post-exam party, bringing you the best bass music Cambridge has to offer.

We've got two low-frequency specialists coming in to tear up the dance - Idioteque and Delian Sound. Idioteque is one of the hottest producers in the UK dubstep scene right now, boasting releases on Sine Audio and the notorious Duploc label. Meanwhile, Delian Sound is one of breakbeat's rising stars, having dropped some of our favourite tracks on Outhouse Sounds in recent months.

The pair will be supported by Murky favourites Avran and Isochrone, as well as a Murky debut for Cambridge's own MillDred. They'll all be bringing you the vibes across a multitude of genres including dubstep, breakbeat, jungle, techno and much, much more.
